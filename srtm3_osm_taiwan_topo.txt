SRTM3.OSM - Taiwan TOPO (by Rudy)
=================================

This is the weekly Taiwan TOPO Garmin Map designed for hiking & geocaching.

這是本週的 SRTM3.OSM - Taiwan TOPO Garmin 地圖，適合用在登山與尋寶！

* SRTM3.OSM.bw - Taiwan TOPO v2016.09.01 (適合小螢幕)
> https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/899714/maps/gmapsupp_Taiwan_zh_bw.img

* SRTM3.OSM.odc - Taiwan TOPO v2016.09.01 (適合大螢幕)
> https://dl.dropboxusercontent.com/u/899714/maps/gmapsupp_Taiwan_zh_odc.img


## Description / 說明

The map combined the most up-to-date (in a week) OSM extracts and NASA SRTMv3 DEM. You could freely download and distributed the map without any charge. But keep in mind that the map contains errors, by using this map you are using it at your own risk. 

這地圖使用目前最新（一個星期內）OSM 的圖資，並結合了藉由 NASA STRMv3 所繪出的等高線圖。你可以隨意的下載或傳給別人使用。並請特別注意，這地圖有錯誤的地方，作者無法保證其正確性。你藉由使用這地圖，你願意自行負責所有可能發生的損失（包括受傷）。
